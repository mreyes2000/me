Chuspa: A Life Cycle Assesment Barcode Scanning App

Environmental Purpose

Whenever I go shopping with my friends or my mom, I can easily recognize whether a product is vegan, cruelty-free, made out of virgin plastic, or imported, and I sort of can recognize what products naturally have large carbon, water, and land footprints.

Unfortunately, I have noticed that people hardly think about the environmental footprint of their shopping habits. Suggesting my friends and family to buy eco-friendly alternatives and use less plastic in their shopping cart showed me that if I could give everyone specific suggestions of eco-friendly shopping alternatives, I could make a positive impact in reducing people’s ecological footprint.

In this project, I designed environmental footprint indices, developed software, interface in Swift, database in Firebase, and styled the visuals of the app. After these components were fully designed and interlinked, we asked our environmental club members to help us fill our online database by researching the inputs for our carbon and water footprints index functions for each item, scanning and classifying items in the supermarket, and writing specific environmental pros and cons for each item.
