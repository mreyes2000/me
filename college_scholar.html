Software Solutions at College Scholar

When I graduated from high school and left to study at Harvard, I felt a great amount of gratitude for my country and school for nurturing me and giving me the tools to be able to study abroad. In order to give back to my community, I founded a college counseling company that would provide college counseling services to the wealthier classes and use the profits to subsidize college counseling services for high achieving low income students so they can have an opportunity to study abroad.

From the counseling and management side, we have employed more than 20 counselors, completed more than 50 college counseling processes and subsidized more than 5 lower income students who have been able to attain full ride scholarships at universities like Northwestern, Columbia, NYU Abu Dhabi, and more. In addition, our students have made it into all 8 ivy league schools as well as Stanford, MIT, UChigago, and more.

From the technical side, I developed the webpge and student portals in Wordpress. I set up the entire domain and cloud infrastructure so that we could have a website and a student portal, which I made by adding PHP enhancements to a basic theme. I connected our portal to our google drive so we could automate processes such as scheduling meetings and adding them onto our calendars, linking students into our portal and generating their folder with document templates, chat group, and zoom links, sending notifications and HTML/CSS email receipts, reporting our results into our database which has functions that use the Google query language and app scripts to make visualizations for our monthly administrative meetings, sending feedback surveys to counselors and students every two months and analyzing the results.
