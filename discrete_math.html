Discrete Math Explorable Explanations

RShiny Apps

Discrete Math is a very abstract topic that is hard to learn by reading descriptions of algorithms and objects through textbooks. Therefore, I built a couple of RShiny apps that calculate permutations, operations, conversions, subgroups, and Cayley graphs of different important groups. Some of the apps also show visualizations of Euclid’s GCD, Fleury’s, Prim’s, and Kruskal’s algorithms.
